Documents

Documents are the content repository of the website. A document can include other document to be rendered when user is browsing the article. These are depending on the article.

Articles

These are the elements that behave like some sort of directory. These are depending on menus. Article are also depending on deadlines. This to avoid browsing with an URL placed in the navigation bar. This avoid rendering of pages wich have a related menu that is offline.

Categories

The menus define the article associated with a menu. These are depending on deadlines.

Deadlines

Deadlines are the elements that controls the display of menu entries and also access to articles. These define a period of time in which the article or the menu is accessible.
